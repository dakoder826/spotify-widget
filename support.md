# Support - Spotify Add Widget

## How to Use
1. Login with your Spotify account
2. Select your preferred playlist in "My Playlists"
3. Add the widget to your lock screen
4. Tap the + button when music is playing

## Troubleshooting
- **Widget not working?** Make sure you're logged into Spotify and music is playing
- **Can't see playlists?** Check your Spotify account has playlists you own or collaborate on
- **Login issues?** Try logging out and back in
